A taste of Colombia in Stockholm

At Kaffe Colombia Express, tradition and craftsmanship come together.
Nestled in Hökarängen, we serve home-style Colombian dishes, Latin American pastries, and carefully curated specialty goods.
A welcoming space where every sip and bite tells a story.

Menu

Explore a menu rooted in tradition—comforting home-cooked meals, delicate pastries, and hearty dishes like Bandeja Paisa. Every plate is a tribute to the rich culinary heritage of Colombia.
Discover the full menu here.

Products

We carefully select and import a range of regional specialties—from rich yerba mate from Uruguay and Argentina to handcrafted Mexican sauces, fine flours, and Chilean confections—our selection brings Latin America's finest ingredients to your table.
