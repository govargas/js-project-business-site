About Us

At Kaffe Colombia Express, Colombian cuisine meets tradition. Nestled in Hökarängen, south of Stockholm, we serve artisanal coffee, Latin American pastries, and specialty goods. A warm, inviting space to enjoy authentic flavors and handcrafted delicacies.

Menu

Explore the flavors of Colombia through a menu rooted in tradition. From delicate pastries to hearty Bandeja Paisa, each dish is crafted with care and authenticity. Let every bite take you on a culinary journey.

Products

We carefully select a range of regional specialties—from rich yerba mate from Uruguay and Argentina to artisanal sauces from Mexico, traditional flours, or sweet indulgences from Chile. Discover the flavors that connect cultures, crafted for those who appreciate authenticity.
