A truly Colombian experience

At Kaffe Colombia Express, Colombian cuisine meets tradition.
Nestled in Hökarängen, south of Stockholm, we serve artisanal coffee, Latin American pastries, and specialty goods.
A warm, inviting space to enjoy authentic flavors and handcrafted delicacies.

Menu

Explore the flavors of Colombia through a menu rooted in tradition. From delicate pastries to hearty Bandeja Paisa, each dish is crafted with care and authenticity. Let every bite take you on a culinary journey.
